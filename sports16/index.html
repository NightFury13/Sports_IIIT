Recent Works

The volleyball court in IIIT campus is now surrounded with a steel wire-framed fence, which is approximately 20-24 ft high. It also has floodlights installed in all directions possible so that it is convenient to play / practice in the night, if need be.

The basketball court in IIIT campus is now surrounded with a steel wire-framed fence, which is approximately 20-24 ft high. It also has floodlights installed and is repainted recently. It had been painted nicely before, when the fence was installed.

Cricket nets have been made in order to make facilities for practicing batting and bowling available to the cricket enthusiasts. Badminton courts have been developed and maintained, though these courts are outdoor courts.

About Us

Sports is an universal language that can bring people together, no matter what their origin, background, religious belief or economic status.

-- Kofi Anan

Sports is a unique activity that infuses the best qualities in any human being. A sports field is considered a laboratory where sports activities groom the individuals physically, mentally and morally with the important values of life to face the world confidently. The values imbibed elicit the best character, behavior and action of an individual, which bring happiness to the individual, the family, the organization and the society.

With this spirit Department of Physical Education and Sports Sciences, IIIT - Hyderabad started its journey in 1999.

Key activities include Physical Training course (Morning PT from 0615 to 0715 hours); Coaching in Football, Hockey, Cricket, Basketball, Volleyball, Kho-Kho, Table Tennis, Badminton, Chess, Martial Arts; Fitness in the evenings from 1700 to 1900 hours. IIIT has a gym that is equipped with multiple weight dumbells, fitness machines and also has modern equipment. It is open in the mornings from 0530 to 0830 hours and in the evenings from 1700 to 2100 hours.

IIIT-H Sports calendar includes Inter House (College has four houses, namely : Aakash, Agni, Prithvi and Vayu) tournaments, Inter Batch tournaments, Premier Leagues, Friendly Matches, Inter College Meets, Sports Carnivals, Annual Athletic Meets, Sports Seminars, University Sports, National Festivals and Adventure Sports. To facilitate a more enthusiastic enjoyment of sports, the Physical Education department, with great support from the Institute, has developed infrastructures like Cricket Nets, Badminton Courts, Basketball and Volleyball Courts with flood lights and is planning to develop such infrastructures in future. With this continued support from everyone, the department is planning to develop a football grass field, synthetic lawn tennis court and an indoor stadium in the near future.
